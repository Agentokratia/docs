## What is the Agent Marketplace?

The Agent Marketplace is a platform for deploying, discovering, and monetizing AI agents. It enables:

- **Agent Deployment** - Deploy your AI agents with built-in payment infrastructure
- **Agent Discovery** - Find and use agents built by others
- **Monetization** - Earn revenue from your agents via x402 payments
